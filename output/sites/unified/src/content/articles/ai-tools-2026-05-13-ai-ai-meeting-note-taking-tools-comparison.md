## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the most accurate AI meeting note-taking tool?

**Microsoft Teams Premium with Copilot** achieves the highest transcription accuracy at 96.1% according to independent benchmarks, followed by Otter.ai at 95.2%. However, accuracy varies significantly based on audio quality, speaker accents, and background noise. For enterprise users already invested in Microsoft 365, the marginal accuracy difference (less than 1%) may not justify switching platforms, especially considering Teams Copilot's superior integration with productivity tools like Word, Outlook, and OneDrive.

### Which AI meeting tool offers the best value for small teams?

**Fireflies.ai** offers the best value for small teams at $10/user/month for unlimited recordings and 8,000 minutes of storage. However, **Zoom AI Companion** provides the lowest total cost of ownership for teams already paying for Zoom subscriptions, as it requires no additional per-user fees. If your team conducts 20+ meetings monthly and doesn't need advanced CRM integrations, the free tier of Otter.ai (300 minutes/month) or Fireflies.ai (800 minutes/month) may be sufficient for teams under 5 people.

### Can these tools transcribe meetings in real-time during video calls?

All major AI meeting tools now offer real-time transcription capabilities. **Zoom AI Companion** provides the fastest processing at under 20 seconds due to native integration, while **Otter.ai** and **Fireflies.ai** process in 30-45 seconds respectively through their browser extensions or integrations. **Microsoft Teams Copilot** delivers real-time transcription but requires a 5-minute processing delay for PSTN phone calls. **Fellow.app** currently only supports post-meeting transcription, making it unsuitable for real-time note-taking during meetings.

### How do AI meeting tools handle privacy and data security?

Enterprise-grade security varies significantly: **Microsoft Teams Copilot** offers the most comprehensive compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, and FedRAMP certifications, plus data residency options for EU customers. **Otter.ai** and **Fireflies.ai** both offer SOC 2 Type II compliance, AES-256 encryption, and GDPR adherence, with data stored in US-based AWS data centers by default. **Zoom AI Companion** provides end-to-end encryption and recently achieved ISO 27001 certification. When evaluating enterprise plans, request each vendor's data processing agreement (DPA) and confirm where transcription data is stored, how long it's retained, and whether human review of content occurs.

## Final Verdict


![Illustration for ai meeting note taking tools comparison](https://picsum.photos/seed/ai-meeting-note-taking-tools-comparison-mid/1200/630)


The "best" AI meeting note-taking tool depends entirely on your organizational context:

**For Sales Teams**: Choose **Fireflies.ai** for its superior CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho), deal intelligence features, and pipeline analytics. The $19/user/month Business plan pays for itself through improved follow-up accuracy and conversation intelligence tracking. Fireflies.ai's 94.8% transcription accuracy is sufficient for capturing client requirements accurately.

**For Enterprise Microsoft 365 Organizations**: Select **Microsoft Teams Premium with Copilot** at $22/user/month. The investment is justified by native integration with Outlook, Word, and OneDrive, enabling automatic meeting notes that sync directly to your existing document workflows. Organizations already paying for Microsoft 365 Business Premium ($22/user/month) effectively receive Copilot at no additional per-feature cost.

**For Collaboration-Focused Teams**: **Otter.ai** remains the gold standard for real-time collaboration, with 95.2% accuracy, simultaneous editing, and the most intuitive interface for non-technical users. The Pro plan at $16.99/user/month includes 6,000 minutes annually, sufficient for most teams conducting 10-15 weekly meetings.

**For Zoom-Native Teams**: **Zoom AI Companion** provides the best value if your organization already subscribes to Zoom Pro or higher. Zero additional per-user costs combined with the fastest transcription processing (under 20 seconds) make this the most cost-effective option for teams exclusively using Zoom for communication.

**For Budget-Conscious Startups**: **Fellow.app** at $10/user/month offers the best price-to-feature ratio for small teams prioritizing agenda preparation and async collaboration over real-time transcription. However, its 92.4% accuracy rate and limited language support (10 languages) may be constraining as your team scales internationally.

**Recommendation Summary**: If you can only choose one tool for a mixed-use environment, **Fireflies.ai** delivers the most versatile platform at competitive pricing. Its combination of 32-language support, 40+ integrations, and strong transcription accuracy makes it suitable for most use cases. For specialized requirements—maximum accuracy (Teams Copilot), collaborative editing (Otter.ai), or native Zoom integration (AI Companion)—evaluate the specific tools in those categories instead.
