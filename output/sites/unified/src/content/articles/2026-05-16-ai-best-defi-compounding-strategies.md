## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the safest DeFi compounding strategy for conservative investors?

Stablecoin LP through Curve's 3pool or Lido's stETH vault represent the lowest-risk compounding options, offering 5-12% APY with minimal impermanent loss. The stablecoin strategy carries depeg risk but avoids volatility exposure. Conservative investors should allocate no more than 20% of crypto holdings to these strategies while maintaining diversified exposure across multiple protocols.

### How often should I compound my DeFi yields?

Most auto-compounding protocols rebalance every 24-48 hours, making manual compounding unnecessary for most strategies. Gas costs above $15 make manual compounding unprofitable for positions under $10,000. Use protocols like Yearn or Beefy that batch transactions to reduce individual gas costs by 60-80% compared to manual compounding. Check protocol-specific optimal compounding intervals—typically 4-12 hours for high-APR positions above $50,000.

### What is the minimum investment required for profitable DeFi farming?

Profitability thresholds depend on gas costs and strategy APR. For Ethereum mainnet, positions above $5,000 typically generate positive returns after fees. Arbitrum and Polygon allow profitable farming starting at $500-1,000 due to lower gas costs (typically $0.10-0.50 per transaction). Cross-chain strategies require $2,000-5,000 minimum to justify bridge fees and multi-protocol complexity. Tools like Zapper and DeBank help calculate exact break-even points for specific strategies.
