### How do I calculate real net APY after fees and gas costs?

Subtract **1–2%** for gas costs on Ethereum mainnet transactions (approximately **$15–30** per transaction). For protocols like Yearn, factor in the **20%** performance fee on profits. The formula is: **Net APY = (Gross APY × 0.98) - (Annual Gas Fees / Principal)**. On L2 networks like Arbitrum or Optimism, gas costs drop to **$0.50–$2 per transaction**, making smaller deposits viable with net APY only **0.2–0.5%** lower than gross figures.
