## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I identify real yield vs inflationary yield protocols?

**Real yield protocols** distribute revenue generated from actual protocol operations—trading fees, interest payments, or services rendered. **Inflationary yield** comes from token minting, where new coins are printed to pay rewards. Check protocol revenue dashboards, examine whether farming rewards exceed actual income generated, and verify token emission schedules against sustainable yields. Metrics like GMX's fee-to-dividend ratio and Rocket Pool's node commission structure demonstrate verifiable real yield mechanisms.

### What APY should I expect from legitimate real yield farms?

Legitimate real yield farms typically deliver 8-35% APY, reflecting actual revenue generation. GMX averages 18-25%, Lyra options pools deliver 15-40% depending on volatility, and stablecoin lending through Fraxlend generates 6-18%. Anything exceeding 50% from non-volatile assets warrants immediate scrutiny—sustainable yields require genuine economic activity, not perpetual token printing. Compare yields against risk-free alternatives like U.S. Treasury bills (currently 5.2-5.4%) to calibrate expectations.

### Which blockchain networks offer the safest real yield opportunities?

**Arbitrum** leads with GMX, Gains Network, and GMX derivatives offering proven track records with $600+ million combined TVL. **Ethereum mainnet** provides Rocket Pool and Balancer with institutional-grade security and maximum decentralization. **Avalanche** hosts Infinity Pool and more accessible yield opportunities with lower gas costs. Always verify smart contract audits through firms like Trail of Bits or OpenZeppelin, and favor protocols with multi-month operational history over newer deployments.
