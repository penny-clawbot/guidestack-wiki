# Off-Season Travel Deals and Tips  

Off-season travel slashes costs by 30‑50 % on flights, hotels, and attractions, while delivering thinner crowds and authentic local experiences. The top deals this year are concentrated in destinations like the Azores, Crete, Bali, Vietnam’s Hoi An, Riviera Maya, Kyoto, Patagonia, Reykjavik, and Marrakech. By targeting these regions during their low‑season windows, you can secure round‑trip flights under $300, nightly accommodation rates as low as $20, and save up to $200 on activities and tours. Below is a curated list of 9 off‑season gems with specific price data, ratings, and insider tips to help you plan a budget‑friendly getaway.

## 1. Azores, Portugal – Late Winter/Early Spring (Nov‑Mar)  


![Hero image for off season travel deals and tips](https://picsum.photos/seed/off-season-travel-deals-and-tips-hero/1200/630)


**Pros**  
- **Affordable flights** – U.S. → Ponta Delgada often under $300 round‑trip (Skyscanner, 2024).  
- **Low accommodation costs** – Median Airbnb price $55/night (Airbnb, 2023).  
- **Stunning nature** with fewer tourists; whale‑watching tours start at $45.  

**Cons**  
- Cooler temperatures (avg. 12 °C/54 °F) and occasional rain.  
- Some attractions have reduced hours in Jan‑Feb.  

**Details**  
- Book flights in early November for the best fares; prices rise ~15 % by December.  
- Stay at **Casa da Avó**, a family‑run guesthouse rated 9.2/10 on Booking.com, for $58/night.  
- Budget $10‑15/day for meals at local *tasquinhas* serving fresh seafood.

## 2. Crete, Greece – Shoulder Season (Apr‑May & Oct‑Nov)  

**Pros**  
- **Flight deals** from the U.S. average $210 round‑trip (Google Flights, 2023).  
- **Hotel discounts** – 4‑star beachfront properties drop to $68/night (Booking.com, 2024).  
- Warm weather (20‑25 °C/68‑77 °F) perfect for sightseeing ancient ruins.  

**Cons**  
- Ferry services to smaller islands are less frequent in Oct.  
- Some coastal restaurants close after mid‑Oct.  

**Details**  
- Visit the **Palace of Knossos** in early May; ticket price €15 (≈ $16) and no queues.  
- Opt for a centrally located apartment in **Rethymno** costing $55/night (Airbnb).  
- TripAdvisor rating for Crete’s top sites: 4.6/5 (2023).

## 3. Bali, Indonesia – Off‑Season (Oct‑Dec)  


![Illustration for off season travel deals and tips](https://picsum.photos/seed/off-season-travel-deals-and-tips-mid/1200/630)


**Pros**  
- **Lowest hotel rates** – Average $30/night for 3‑star hotels (Hotels.com, 2023).  
- Lush rice terraces after early rains, fewer crowds at temples.  
- Round‑trip flights from the U.S. from $450 (Expedia, 2024).  

**Cons**  
- Monsoon rains can be heavy, especially in Dec.  
- Some tour operators reduce schedules in late Dec.  

**Details**  
- Book a **Ubud jungle villa** for $35/night (Airbnb, 2024) and enjoy complimentary yoga sessions.  
- Save on transport: Grab bike rental $5/day.  
- Overall TripAdvisor rating for Bali’s top experiences: 4.7/5 (2023).
