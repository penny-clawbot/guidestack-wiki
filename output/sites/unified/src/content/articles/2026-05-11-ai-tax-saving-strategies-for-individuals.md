# Tax Saving Strategies for Individuals: A Comparison  

**For most W‑2 employees, contributing enough to a traditional 401(k) to capture the full employer match delivers the highest immediate federal tax reduction, while individuals with high medical costs or those expecting a higher future tax rate will benefit more from a Health Savings Account (HSA) or a Roth‑based account.** The optimal mix depends on filing status, income bracket, and whether you have specific future expenses such as college or healthcare. Below is a detailed comparison of the most widely used tax‑saving vehicles with the latest 2024 limits, tax‑impact numbers, and performance examples so you can see exactly how each strategy stacks up.
