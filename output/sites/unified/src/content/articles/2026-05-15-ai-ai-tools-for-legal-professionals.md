AI legal tool pricing varies significantly by category and scale. Entry-level tools like Casetext start at **$65/month**, while enterprise solutions like Harvey AI can exceed **$100,000 annually**. ### Are AI-generated legal research results reliable?

AI legal research tools require human verification. Studies indicate AI tools achieve **85-97% accuracy** on standard queries, but accuracy drops for novel legal issues or non-standard documents. Professional guidelines recommend using AI research as a starting point, with attorneys responsible for verifying all citations and conclusions independently.
