## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which AI tool is best for startups with limited budgets?

**Figma's free tier** is the clear winner for budget-conscious teams. It includes unlimited personal projects, real-time collaboration for up to 2 editors, and access to Figma's AI-powered Auto Layout. Startups can collaborate with up to 30-minute sessions for free. For research, **Uizard's $29/month pro plan** offers the best value, generating 50 AI designs monthly with full export capabilities. Combine these two tools for under $30/month total to cover both design and rapid prototyping needs.

### How much time do AI tools actually save in the UX workflow?

Based on aggregated user studies and vendor data:
- **Design phase**: AI tools reduce time by 40-60% (Figma, Sketch)
- **Prototyping phase**: Up to 10x faster with Framer AI and Uizard
- **User research analysis**: 3-5x faster with Maze and UserTesting AI
- **Overall project timeline**: 25-35% reduction from concept to handoff

A typical 2-week design sprint can be compressed to 10-12 days using AI-assisted workflows, according to Nielsen Norman Group research.

### Do AI tools replace traditional UX design skills?

**No, but they shift the skill emphasis.** AI tools handle repetitive tasks like resizing, alignment, and basic research analysis, but UX designers must still:
- Define user problems and research goals
- Make strategic design decisions
- Interpret AI-generated insights critically
- Validate designs with real users
- Communicate findings to stakeholders

The most valuable skills in an AI-augmented workflow are **system thinking, strategic research design, and critical analysis**—not software proficiency.

### What integration capabilities matter most for AI UX tools?

**API access and plugin ecosystems** are critical for professional workflows. Figma leads with 1,000+ plugins and robust REST API supporting custom automation. For research tools, **Maze and UserTesting offer Zapier integrations** connecting to 5,000+ apps. Key integrations to prioritize:
- **Design handoff**: Inspect and developer handoff tools
- **Project management**: Jira, Asana, Monday.com sync
- **Version control**: GitHub/Bitbucket for design tokens
- **Analytics**: Amplitude, Mixpanel integration for post-launch validation
