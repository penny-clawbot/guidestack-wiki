## What Is the Ichimoku Cloud Strategy?


![Hero image for ichimoku cloud crypto trading](https://picsum.photos/seed/ichimoku-cloud-crypto-trading-hero/1200/630)


The Ichimoku Cloud (Ichimoku Kinko Hyo) is a technical analysis indicator created by Japanese journalist Goichi Hosoda in 1969, originally for the rice market. In crypto trading, it combines multiple moving averages to create a "cloud" that visualizes support, resistance, and trend direction simultaneously.

The five core components include:

- **Tenkan-sen (Conversion Line):** 9-period moving average
- **Kijun-sen (Base Line):** 26-period moving average  
- **Senkou Span A (Leading Span A):** Average of Tenkan and Kijun, projected 26 periods ahead
- **Senkou Span B (Leading Span B):** 52-period moving average, projected 26 periods ahead
- **Chikou Span (Lagging Span):** Current price plotted 26 periods backward

The cloud forms between Senkou Span A and B, with colors indicating bullish (green/teal) or bearish (red/pink) sentiment.
