## How do AI recommendation engines work?

AI recommendation engines analyze user behavior data through collaborative filtering, content-based filtering, and hybrid approaches. Collaborative filtering examines what similar users purchased or viewed to suggest products, while content-based filtering matches product attributes to user preferences. Netflix reported in 2026 that their recommendation AI saves $1 billion annually in customer retention. Real-time processing engines like Amazon Personalize can update recommendations within 30 milliseconds of user interaction.
