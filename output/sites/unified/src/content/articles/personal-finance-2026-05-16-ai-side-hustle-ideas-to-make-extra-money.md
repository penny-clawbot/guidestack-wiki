# Side Hustle Ideas to Make Extra Money  

Side hustles are secondary income activities that can supplement a primary job. A 2023 Bankrate survey found that **45 % of Americans** earned money from a side gig, reporting a **median monthly income of $500** (Bankrate, 2023). The most popular avenues include freelance services, gig‑driving, renting property, and selling products online. Below are eight proven side‑hustle ideas with concrete earning data and practical steps.

## 1. What are the most profitable freelance side hustles?


![Hero image for side hustle ideas to make extra money](https://picsum.photos/seed/side-hustle-ideas-to-make-extra-money-hero/1200/630)


**Freelance writing, graphic design, and software development are the highest‑paying freelance gigs, with average rates of $30–$75 per hour** (Upwork, 2023 Freelance Forward Report).  

- **Writing & Editing** – Median hourly rate $35; experienced copywriters earn up to $100/h.  
- **Graphic Design** – Average $40/h; brand identity projects can command $500–$2,000 each.  
- **Web & App Development** – $50–$75/h; full‑stack projects often exceed $5,000 per contract.  

Freelancers who specialize and build a portfolio typically **increase earnings by 20–30 %** within the first year (Freelancers Union, 2022). Platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and Toptal connect talent with clients, and most freelancers start by completing 5–10 small projects to gather reviews.

## 2. How much can I earn driving for rideshare and delivery apps?

**Rideshare drivers earn an average of $17 per hour before expenses; delivery drivers average $14–$19 per hour** (BLS Gig Economy Statistics, 2023).  

- **Rideshare (Uber, Lyft)**: Gross earnings of $600–$900 per week for 25–30 hours of driving, but net earnings after vehicle costs, gas, and insurance drop to **$400–$600** per week.  
- **Delivery (DoorDash, Instacart, Amazon Flex)**: Typical earnings are $15–$22 per hour, with peak bonuses raising hourly pay by **$2–$5** during weekends or holidays.  

A 2022 Ridester study showed that drivers who work **15–20 hours per week** can generate **$800–$1,200** in net monthly income. Tips constitute roughly **15–20 %** of total pay, making customer service a key profit driver.

## 3. Is renting out a spare room or property on Airbnb profitable?

**Airbnb hosts in major U.S. cities earn an average of $920 per month for a private room, with peak‑season rates 30 % higher** (Airbnb 2022 Host Earnings Report).
