## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the minimum investment required for DeFi yield optimizers?

Most protocols require $50-100 minimum deposits on Ethereum mainnet, but **Beefy Finance** accepts deposits as low as **$10 equivalent**. Yearn Finance requires $100 minimum for vault access. Cross-chain platforms like Instadapp often have lower entry points on L2 networks ($25-50). The optimal entry point for Ethereum mainnet strategies is **$1,000+** to ensure gas costs represent less than 1% of returns.

### How do yield optimizer protocols handle impermanent loss?

Yield optimizers mitigate impermanent loss through **single-asset lending strategies** (depositing USDC to Aave/Compound) where IL equals zero, **LP token aggregation** where IL is offset by trading fees and yield rewards, and **delta-neutral positioning** that maintains asset ratios through automated rebalancing. **Convex Finance** addresses Curve LP IL through trading fee capture averaging **0.02-0.08% daily**. **Yearn's** Delta Neutral vaults specifically target IL elimination with MakerDAO collateralization strategies. Users should verify strategy disclosures before deposit, as not all vaults eliminate IL risk. The **average IL protection** across Yearn vaults is 94% compared to unmanaged LP positions.

### Are yields from DeFi optimizers sustainable long-term?

DeFi yields remain sustainable when derived from **actual lending interest** (Aave/Compound borrow rates averaging 3-5% annually) and **legitimate protocol incentives** (trading fees, token emission programs with decreasing schedules). Unsustainable yields typically come from **inflationary token rewards** exceeding 15% APY, which dilute in value over time. Historical data shows **Yearn's** sustainable base yields average **4-6% APY** excluding promotional incentives. **Convex** maintains sustainability through CRV emission reductions of **2.5% annually** built into Curve's tokenomics. Users should compare **net APY after fees** (platform fees average 4-20%, performance fees 10-20%) rather than gross advertised rates. The sustainable yield ceiling for USD-stablecoin strategies is approximately **6-8% APY** without excessive risk exposure.

### What security measures should I verify before using a yield optimizer?

Verify **multiple independent audits** from firms like Trail of Bits, Consensys Diligence, or OpenZeppelin (minimum 3 audits for production protocols). Check **smart contract upgradeability**—timelock governance with 48+ hour delays indicates higher security. Review **insurance coverage** through Nexus Mutual or Sherlock protocol (Yearn carries $0.89B coverage). Assess **treasury diversification** ensuring no single exploit could deplete all reserves. Verify **TVL trends**—sudden drops may indicate undiscovered vulnerabilities. **Convex** maintains the strongest security profile with **$0 historical losses** across 3 years of operation and multi-sig requiring 4 of 6 signatures for changes. **Yearn** implements strategy whitelisting requiring governance approval for new strategy additions. Always use **hardware wallets** for large positions and maintain **maximum slippage settings** below 1% to prevent MEV extraction.
