Memecoin prices correlate strongly with broader crypto market cycles, and understanding these phases is crucial for timing your entry. The most favorable conditions occur when Ethereum gas fees drop below 30 gwei, signaling reduced speculative activity and potentially undervalued memecoin opportunities.
