### How do I calculate real DeFi staking returns after fees?

Subtract platform fees from gross APY: for Lido's 4.5% gross yield minus 0.5% fee and 10% validator commission equals approximately 3.65% net APY. Account for token inflation if staking inflationary assets like ATOM or DOT, where 10% inflation on a 15% gross yield results in 5% real yield. Factor in gas costs—ETH staking rewards of $100 annually become unprofitable if transaction fees exceed that threshold.

### Which DeFi staking offers the highest guaranteed APY?

**Aave stablecoin lending currently guarantees 4.2-5.1% APY** on USDC/USDT with no lock-up periods. However, yields fluctuate based on utilization rates—when crypto markets crash and borrowing demand drops, yields decrease proportionally. Cosmos ATOM staking guarantees 8% minimum under the dynamic inflation model that adjusts rewards based on staking participation rates.

### What are the tax implications of DeFi staking rewards?

Staking rewards are generally treated as ordinary income at receipt in most jurisdictions (US, UK, Germany, Canada) based on the token's fair market value when received. Subsequent appreciation is taxed as capital gains upon disposal. Ethereum's transition to proof-of-stake means staking rewards qualify as income—previously, proof-of-work miners faced different treatment. Most jurisdictions require annual reporting regardless of reinvestment.
