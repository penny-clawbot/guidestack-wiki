## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are AI mental health tools a replacement for human therapy?

No. AI tools supplement but cannot replace professional mental health care. They work best for mild-to-moderate support, daily check-ins, and skill-building. For clinical depression, suicidal ideation, or complex trauma, licensed therapists and psychiatrists remain essential. Apps like Woebot and Wysa explicitly state they are "tools to support—not replace—professional care."

### How private and secure is my data when using these apps?

Most reputable mental health apps comply with HIPAA (in the US) and GDPR (in Europe). However, data practices vary significantly. Replika stores conversation history for AI memory functions, while Crisis Text Line anonymizes all data for research. Always review privacy policies, opt out of data sharing when possible, and avoid sharing highly sensitive information in AI-only platforms.

### Which AI mental health tool offers the best value for money?

Value depends on your needs. For basic daily support, Woebot's free version delivers evidence-based CBT techniques. For structured therapy with human counselors, BetterHelp ($240–$320/month) provides the most comprehensive care. For meditation and stress relief, Headspace or Calm ($70/year) offer excellent value. Users with insurance should check employer benefits—many cover Talkspace or BetterHelp at reduced costs.
