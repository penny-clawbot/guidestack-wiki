These platforms reduce website development time by up to 70% and have helped create over 200 million websites globally as of 2026, according to website builder market research by MarketsandMarkets.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best AI website builder for beginners?

Wix ADI ranks highest for beginners with its intuitive interface and extensive template library, scoring 4.5/5 in PCMag's 2026 evaluation. GoDaddy Airo offers the simplest setup process requiring minimal user input. Bookmark provides the fastest initial website generation at under 5 minutes. The best choice depends on specific needs: Wix for design flexibility, Durable for speed, or Shopify for e-commerce integration.

### Can AI website builders handle e-commerce functionality?

Yes, major AI platforms including Shopify, Wix, and Squarespace offer full e-commerce capabilities. Shopify reports processing over $200 billion in merchant transactions through its platform. AI features include automated product descriptions, pricing recommendations, and inventory management. Transaction fees and payment processing integration vary by platform, with most charging 2.9% + 30¢ per sale.

AI website builders prioritize security through automated SSL certificates, DDoS protection, and regular security updates.
