Taxation varies by jurisdiction—U.S. users must report yield farming rewards as ordinary income at receipt, with capital gains applied upon token disposition. The IRS classified DeFi staking as property income in 2024, requiring accurate cost-basis tracking for every reward received.

### Can you lose money in yield farming?
