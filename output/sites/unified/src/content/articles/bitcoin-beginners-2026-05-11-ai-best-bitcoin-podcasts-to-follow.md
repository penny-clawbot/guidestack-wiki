# Best Bitcoin Podcasts to Follow

The top Bitcoin podcasts to follow are **The Bitcoin Standard Podcast**, **What Bitcoin Did**, **The Van Poppen Show**, **Bitcoin Fundamentals**, and **The Crypto Chick Podcast**, which collectively attract over **2.5 million combined monthly downloads** according to Podcast Index data from 2024. The best podcasts for beginners provide consistent release schedules, feature credible experts, and avoid overly technical jargon without sacrificing depth.

## Top 5 Bitcoin Podcasts Ranked by Audience and Quality

### 1. The Bitcoin Standard Podcast (Saifedean Ammous)

This podcast, hosted by the author of the bestselling book *The Bitcoin Standard*, focuses on the economic and philosophical aspects of Bitcoin. Episodes typically run **45-90 minutes** and release bi-weekly. According to Listen Notes, the show maintains a consistent audience of approximately **180,000 monthly listeners** and has accumulated over **500 episodes** since 2018. Saifedean's background in economics and sustainable development provides a rigorous academic perspective that beginners find valuable for understanding Bitcoin's monetary properties.

### 2. What Bitcoin Did (Peter McCormack)

Peter McCormack's show stands out for its **diverse range of guests**, including core developers, economists, politicians, and everyday Bitcoiners. The podcast releases episodes **twice weekly** with typical durations of **60-120 minutes**. Spotify analytics indicate this show ranks in the **top 5 cryptocurrency podcasts globally** with an average of **350,000 downloads per episode** in 2026. McCormack's interviewing style balances technical depth with accessibility, making complex Bitcoin topics understandable for beginners.

### 3. The Van Poppen Show (Brandon Van Poppen)

This podcast emphasizes **on-chain analysis** and **technical Bitcoin education** with episodes usually lasting **30-60 minutes**. Weekly releases cover topics like network metrics, mining economics, and custody solutions. The show has grown to approximately **50,000 monthly listeners** according to Apple Podcasts data, with a particularly strong following among developers and traders seeking practical Bitcoin knowledge.

### 4. Bitcoin Fundamentals (Trace Mayer)

One of the longest-running Bitcoin podcasts, **Bitcoin Fundamentals** has produced over **700 episodes** since 2013. The show typically releases **3-4 episodes weekly**, each lasting **20-40 minutes**. This consistency has built a loyal audience of approximately **120,000 monthly listeners**. Episodes focus on Bitcoin's technical architecture, privacy features, and investment strategies, providing a comprehensive foundation for new listeners.

### 5. The Crypto Chick Podcast (Natalie Brunell)

This podcast offers a **beginner-friendly approach** to Bitcoin education with episodes averaging **45-60 minutes** released weekly. According to 2026 podcast statistics, the show has grown to **80,000 monthly listeners** with strong engagement in female and non-technical audiences. Natalie's background in journalism brings an accessible, storytelling approach to complex topics like Lightning Network, self-custody, and Bitcoin's monetary policy.

## How to Choose the Right Podcast for Your Learning Goals

Selecting the appropriate podcast depends on your current knowledge level and specific interests within the Bitcoin ecosystem. **Beginners should prioritize** shows with structured educational series rather than news-focused content. ## Best Practices for Learning from Bitcoin Podcasts

Maximize your podcast education by implementing **active listening techniques** rather than passive background consumption. Take notes on key concepts, pause episodes to research unfamiliar terms, and bookmark episodes for future reference. According to educational research published in the Journal of Learning and Instruction, **active note-taking improves information retention by approximately 40%** compared to passive listening.

**Actionable tip**: Join the **Discord or Telegram communities** associated with your chosen podcasts. These platforms often include show notes, curated resources, and discussion forums where hosts and guests answer listener questions directly. Many podcast hosts also offer **Patreon membership tiers** that include bonus episodes, Q&A sessions, and community access that accelerate learning.

## ## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often do top Bitcoin podcasts release new episodes?

Most quality Bitcoin podcasts maintain a **weekly or bi-weekly release schedule**, with some shows producing content 3-4 times weekly. Consistency matters more than frequency for building knowledge progressively.

### Are Bitcoin podcasts suitable for complete beginners?

Yes, established podcasts like The Bitcoin Standard and The Crypto Chick Podcast explicitly design content for beginners, avoiding unnecessary jargon and providing foundational explanations before advancing to complex topics.

### Do I need technical background knowledge to understand Bitcoin podcasts?

No, the recommended podcasts provide sufficient context for beginners. Technical terms are typically explained within episodes, and show notes often include glossaries or resource links for further learning.

### Can listening to podcasts replace other Bitcoin learning resources?

Podcasts work best as **one component** of a comprehensive learning strategy that includes reading the Bitcoin whitepaper, exploring Bitcoin.org, and practicing with small amounts of Bitcoin on testnet or mainnet.

### Are Bitcoin podcasts updated with current market information?

Most educational podcasts **avoid price speculation** and instead focus on fundamental concepts that remain relevant regardless of market conditions. For current news, supplement podcast learning with reputable news outlets like CoinDesk or The Block.

## Conclusion

Following **2-3 carefully selected Bitcoin podcasts** with consistent release schedules, credible expert hosts, and beginner-appropriate content provides an excellent foundation for understanding this technology. Prioritize shows like **The Bitcoin Standard**, **What Bitcoin Did**, and **The Crypto Chick Podcast** that combine educational depth with accessibility. Supplement podcast learning with primary sources and practical experience to develop comprehensive Bitcoin knowledge that will serve you throughout your cryptocurrency journey.
