# Best High‑Yield Savings Accounts: A 2025 Comparison

The **Marcus by Goldman Sachs High‑Yield Savings** offers the highest APY at 5.30 % (as of March 2025) with no monthly fee, no minimum deposit, and a $100 cash bonus for deposits over $10 k—making it the top choice for savers who prioritize maximum return. If you also need fee‑free ATM access and a highly rated mobile app, **Ally Bank** (4.85 % APY) is the best runner‑up. Below is a detailed side‑by‑side look at the leading accounts so you can pick the right fit for your goals.

### Key Takeaways from the Data

- **Marcus** delivers the highest APY (5.30 %) and a generous sign‑up bonus, but has no physical ATM refunds.
- **Ally** ties for the most user‑friendly experience (4.9‑star app) and offers fee‑free ATM access via Allpoint.
- **CIT Bank** is the only account on the list with a $100 minimum deposit, which still beats most traditional banks but may be a barrier for small savers.
- **Synchrony** and **Capital One 360** fall just below the 5 % mark, yet still outperform the national average savings rate of ~0.45 % (FDIC, Feb 2025).
- **Discover** provides the largest cash bonus ($200) but a slightly lower APY (4.70 %).
