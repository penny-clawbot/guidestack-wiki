# Budget Travel Packing List: The Ultimate Guide  

The three highest‑value items for any budget‑minded traveler are the **Osprey Farpoint 40 backpack (~$150, 4.8/5★)**, the **Anker PowerCore 10000 portable charger (~$25, 4.7/5★)**, and the **Eagle Creek Pack‑It Specter packing cubes (~$20, 4.6/5★)**. These picks combine durability, weight savings, and top‑rated performance to keep your gear organized without breaking the bank.

## 1. Osprey Farpoint 40 Travel Backpack  
**Pros**  
- Lightweight (2 lb 2 oz / 0.97 kg) yet sturdy frame with reinforced side panels.  
- Carry‑on compliant (40 L) with front‑panel zip access for easy packing.  
- Lifetime warranty (Osprey’s “All Mighty Guarantee”).  

**Cons**  
- Limited external lash points compared with larger trekking packs.  
- Hip‑belt pockets are small; not ideal for those who need quick‑access storage.  

**Details**  
- **Price:** $149.95 (Amazon, 4.8/5★ from 12,400+ reviews)  
- **Capacity:** 40 L (fits most airline carry‑on limits)  
- **Dimensions:** 22 × 14 × 9 in (56 × 35 × 23 cm)  
- **Material:** 210 D nylon double‑diamond ripstop  
- **Warranty:** Lifetime “All Mighty Guarantee” (covers defects)

**Cons**

- **Capacity:** Half‑cube ≈ 5 L; full‑cube ≈ 10 L; shoe bag ≈ 6 L
