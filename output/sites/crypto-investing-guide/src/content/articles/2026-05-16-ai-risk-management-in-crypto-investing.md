# Risk Management in Crypto Investing: Stop‑Loss vs. Derivatives Hedging

**For most retail investors, a disciplined stop‑loss combined with modest position sizing offers the simplest, most cost‑effective way to limit downside without requiring constant monitoring.**  
**For experienced traders or institutions with access to derivatives markets, a portfolio hedged with options or futures can cut drawdowns more sharply, but it demands higher capital, expertise, and ongoing management.**
