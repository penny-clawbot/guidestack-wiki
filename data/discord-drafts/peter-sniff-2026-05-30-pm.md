**Post 1 — Weekend portfolio review discipline**

Saturday rule: review your positions sober. No FOMO. No panic. Just data.

• What's up? Why? (Fundamental or noise?)
• What's down? Why? (Temporary or structural?)
• Are you sized correctly for Monday's risk?

Most traders lose money on weekends not because markets move, but because they overthink positions they can't even adjust until Monday.

Do the review. Close the tab. Go live your life. The market will still be there.
