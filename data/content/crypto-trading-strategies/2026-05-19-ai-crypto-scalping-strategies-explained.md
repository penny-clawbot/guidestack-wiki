## **Crypto Scalping Strategies You Must Know for Precise, High‑Frequency Trading**

The **best scalping strategies** in the crypto market:

1. **Mean‑Reversion Scalping**  
   - **Pros**:  
     - Quick trades, low exposure, high‑frequency profit capture.
   - **Cons**:  
     - Needs a reliable **RSI/Mean‑Reversion** calculation.
     - **Bollinger Bands** (20‑period SMA) shows price reversion points.

2. **Momentum Burst**  
   - **Pros**:  
     - Large volume spikes **+ volume data** to capture 0.5‑2‑minute candle data.
   - **Cons**:  
     - Quick reversal risk. Use a **price‑action** patterns to confirm.

3. **Order‑Block‑Flip**  
   - **Pros**:  
     - Identify the block where large traders entered and the **order‑block‑flip** entry.
   - **Cons**:  
     - Need to read the **Candlestick‑Level** data.

4. **VWAP‑Cross**  
   - **Pros**:  
     - **VWAP** cross can determine the **order‑blocks** quickly.
