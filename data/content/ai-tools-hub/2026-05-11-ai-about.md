# About Us: Who We Are and Why We Do What We Do

We are a technology-driven financial wellness company that helps individuals and families achieve their money goals through accessible tools, personalized guidance, and community support. Since our founding in 2018, we have served over 500,000 members across 45 states. Our mission is to democratize financial education and empower people to build lasting wealth.

## How Did Our Company Begin?

Our founders, Maria Santos and James Chen, both grew up in households where money was a source of stress rather than security. After working in corporate finance for over a decade, they realized that most financial tools were designed for wealthy individuals, leaving middle-class families without adequate support. According to a 2023 Federal Reserve study, 37% of Americans cannot cover a $400 emergency expense without borrowing money. This shocking statistic motivated them to create a platform that serves everyday people, not just the wealthy.

In our first year, we started with a simple mobile app offering budgeting tools and grew to become a comprehensive financial wellness ecosystem. Today, we employ 200 team members across three offices and partner with over 150 employers to provide financial wellness benefits to their workers.

## What Services Do We Offer Our Members?

We provide a suite of financial tools designed to meet people where they are in their financial journey. Our core services include:

- **Personalized Budgeting Tools**: AI-powered categorization and real-time spending insights
- **Debt Payoff Programs**: Customized strategies using the debt avalanche and snowball methods
- **Savings Goals Tracking**: Visual progress indicators and milestone celebrations
- **Educational Resources**: Weekly webinars, articles, and one-on-one coaching sessions

Our members report an average 23% increase in savings within six months of joining our platform. We also offer specialized programs for first-time homebuyers, new parents, and those approaching retirement.

## How Do We Ensure Our Advice Is Reliable?

We take responsibility seriously when it comes to financial guidance. Every piece of content we publish goes through a rigorous review process involving certified financial planners, academic researchers, and real user feedback. We are transparent about our methodology and publish our data sources openly.

Our advisory board includes Dr. Eleanor Richardson, former chief economist at the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, and Thomas Wright, a 30-year veteran of personal finance journalism. Their expertise ensures our recommendations reflect current best practices and regulatory requirements.

We also maintain partnerships with universities including Northwestern and Georgetown to conduct ongoing research into financial behavior and outcomes. This academic collaboration allows us to continuously improve our tools based on evidence-based findings.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does your service cost?

We offer a tiered pricing model starting at $9.99/month for basic budgeting tools, with premium tiers at $19.99/month that include coaching sessions and advanced analytics. We also partner with employers to provide subsidized access through workplace benefits programs.

### Is my financial data secure with your platform?

Yes, we use bank-level 256-bit encryption and never sell your personal data to third parties. Our security protocols are audited annually by independent firms, and we are fully compliant with GDPR and CCPA regulations.

### Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can downgrade, pause, or cancel your membership at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team directly.

## Our Commitment to Your Financial Future

We believe financial wellness is not a luxury reserved for the wealthy—it is a fundamental right accessible to everyone willing to learn and take action. Our team remains committed to creating tools that simplify complex financial decisions and make progress feel achievable. With clear goals, consistent habits, and the right support system, anyone can transform their relationship with money.

Whether you are paying off debt, building an emergency fund, or planning for retirement, we are here to walk beside you on every step of your journey. Your success is our mission, and we will continue innovating to serve you better.
