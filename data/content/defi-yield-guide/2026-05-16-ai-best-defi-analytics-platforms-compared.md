# Best DeFi Analytics Platforms Compared

For retail DeFi participants seeking an intuitive, free portfolio tracker with real‑time updates, **DeBank** is the best choice. For developers, analysts, and DeFi researchers who need custom SQL dashboards and on‑chain raw data, **Dune Analytics** delivers the deepest query engine and most flexible visualization tools. For institutional investors or fund managers who require wallet‑level labeling, premium alerts, and dedicated support, **Nansen** is the premium solution despite its higher price tag.

### Key Takeaways from the Table

- **Coverage breadth**: Dune Analytics and DappRadar lead with the highest number of indexed contracts and DApps respectively.
- **Refresh latency**: DeBank offers the fastest on‑chain sync for individual wallets, while Dune Analytics provides near‑real‑time data for Pro subscribers.
- **Pricing**: DeBank, DappRadar, and Token Terminal have the lowest entry cost (free to $19/month). Nansen is the most expensive but offers deep wallet labeling.
- **API & Customization**: Dune Analytics stands out with a full SQL API and community‑driven “Spellbook”. Nansen’s API is robust but costs are high.
