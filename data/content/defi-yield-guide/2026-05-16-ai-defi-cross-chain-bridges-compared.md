# DeFi Cross‑Chain Bridges Compared

Cross‑chain bridges are the backbone of DeFi interoperability, and the most cost‑effective options for moving assets between Ethereum and Layer‑2 networks are native L2 bridges (Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon) with average fees under $0.30, while multi‑chain DEX bridges like Stargate and Celer cBridge dominate TVL (≈$2.5 B combined) and offer liquidity across 10+ chains. For users needing the best route across many networks, aggregator meta‑bridges such as Li.Fi and Bungee reduce slippage by up to 30 % compared with direct bridge usage.

## 1. Layer‑2 Native Bridges: Speed, Cost, and TVL  

- **Arbitrum One**  
  - TVL: **$2.3 B** (DeFiLlama, Jan 2025)  
  - Average tx fee: **$0.18** (Ethereum gas ≈ 30 gwei)  
  - Daily transactions: ~**50 k** (Dune Analytics, Q4 2024)  
  - Security: 3 independent audits (Trail of Bits, OpenZeppelin, Consensys Diligence) and a **$100 M** community‑run insurance fund.

- **Optimism**  
  - TVL: **$1.9 B** (DeFiLlama, Jan 2025)  
  - Average tx fee: **$0.12** (gas ≈ 20 gwei)  
  - Daily transactions: ~**30 k** (Dune, Q4 2024)  
  - Insurance fund: **$100 M** (Optimism Collective reserve).
