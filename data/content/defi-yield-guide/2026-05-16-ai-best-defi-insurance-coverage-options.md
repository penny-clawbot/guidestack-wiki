# Best DeFi Insurance Coverage Options  

The most reliable DeFi insurance providers are **Nexus Mutual**, **InsurAce**, and **Cover Protocol**, offering coverage limits ranging from **$500 k to $5 M** and annual premiums as low as **0.5 %**. These platforms collectively protect over **$1.2 B** in TVL, have settled **>95 %** of claims within 72 hours, and are rated **≥4.7/5** on DeFiScore (2024 data from DeFi Llama & DeFiScore).

---

## 1. Nexus Mutual  

**Pros**  
- **High coverage cap**: up to **$5 M** per policy.  
- **Dynamic premium model**: annual rates start at **0.5 %** for low‑risk protocols.  
- **Decentralized governance**: NXM token holders vote on claims.  

**Cons**  
- Premiums can rise sharply for newer or untested smart contracts.  
- Requires KYC for users above a $150 k coverage threshold.  

**Key Details**  
- **Supported protocols**: 120+ (including Aave, Compound, Uniswap).  
- **TVL insured**: **≈$820 M** (DeFi Llama, Jan 2024).  
- **Claim payout speed**: 48‑72 hours after approval.  
- **Rating**: **4.9/5** on DeFiScore (2024).  
- **Minimum policy**: **$10 k** of coverage.  

---

## 2. InsurAce  

**Pros**  
- **Flat‑rate premiums**: **0.75 %** per year for most DeFi protocols.  
- **Multi‑chain support**: covers Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, and Arbitrum.  
- **Quick onboarding**: no KYC for policies under $100 k.  

**Cons**  
- Coverage caps max out at **$1 M** per protocol.  
- Limited coverage for exotic or custom‑coded contracts.  

**Key Details**  
- **Protocol count**: 70+ (e.g., SushiSwap, PancakeSwap).  
- **TVL insured**: **≈$250 M** (InsurAce Dashboard, Q1 2024).  
- **Claim settlement**: average **60 hours**.  
- **User rating**: **4.7/5** (Trustpilot, 2024).  
- **Minimum premium**: **$75** (for $10 k coverage).  

---

## 3. Cover Protocol  

**Pros**  
- **Flexible coverage periods**: weekly, monthly, or annual policies.  
- **No KYC** for any coverage amount.  
- **Community‑driven pricing**: premiums set by risk pools.  

**Cons**  
- Premiums can be volatile based on pool liquidity.  
- Lower coverage caps (**max $500 k**) compared to larger players.  

**Key Details**  
- **Covered assets**: 50+ DeFi assets (including LINK, UNI).  
- **TVL insured**: **≈$130 M** (Cover Protocol, Feb 2024).  
- **Average premium**: **1.2 %** annually.  
- **Claim speed**: <48 hours for 90 % of cases.  
- **Rating**: **4.6/5** on DeFiScore.
