## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Define Investment Goals and Risk Tolerance (Q1 2026)

1. **Set clear objectives** – Decide whether you seek short‑term gains (e.g., 30‑day flips) or longer‑term exposure (6‑12 months).  
2. **Quantify risk tolerance** – For a typical retail investor, allocate no more than 5‑10 % of total investable assets to memecoins.  
3. **Document targets** – Write down a target return (e.g., 50 % in 12 months) and a maximum drawdown limit (e.g., 20 %).
