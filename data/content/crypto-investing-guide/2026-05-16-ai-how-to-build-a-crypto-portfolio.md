# How to Build a Crypto Portfolio: 9 Steps for a Balanced, High‑Growth Strategy

The best assets to anchor a diversified crypto portfolio are **Bitcoin (BTC)** and **Ethereum (ETH)**, supplemented by a mix of layer‑1 altcoins like **Binance Coin (BNB)**, **Cardano (ADA)**, and **Solana (SOL)**. These five coins combine high liquidity, strong security, and proven growth, with BTC trading at $61,500 and ETH at $3,450 as of June 2024 (CoinGecko). By adding smaller‑cap projects such as **Polkadot (DOT)**, **Chainlink (LINK)**, **Avalanche (AVAX)**, and **Polygon (MATIC)**, you can capture upside potential while keeping risk manageable.

## 1. Bitcoin (BTC): The Anchor of Your Portfolio
- **Pros**  
  - Market‑dominant store‑of‑value with the highest liquidity (≈$30 B daily volume).  
  - Institutional adoption (e.g., Spot Bitcoin ETFs approved in 2024) adds credibility.  
  - Proven long‑term ROI: +45 % over the past 12 months (June 2023–June 2024).  
- **Cons**  
  - Volatility still high; price swings of 5–10 % in a day are common.  
  - Energy‑intensive consensus mechanism raises ESG concerns.  
- **Key Data**  
  - Price: $61,500 (June 2024)  
  - Market Cap: $1.2 T  
  - 24h Volume: $29.8 B  
  - CoinGecko Trust Score: **4.9/5**  
- **Recommended Allocation**  
  - **40 %** of total portfolio for core exposure and risk hedge.

## 2. Ethereum (ETH): The Smart‑Contract Backbone
- **Pros**  
  - Leading platform for DeFi, NFTs, and enterprise solutions.  
  - Transition to Proof‑of‑Stake reduced energy use by ≈99 % and improves issuance dynamics.
