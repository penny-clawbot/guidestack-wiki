## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the minimum portfolio size for automated rebalancing to be cost-effective?

Automated rebalancing becomes cost-effective when your portfolio exceeds **$2,500**. Below this threshold, monthly subscription costs (typically $7.99-$19/month) can consume **3-8% of annual returns**. For portfolios under $1,000, manual rebalancing or free-tier tools like CoinStats' basic plan are more efficient. Shrimpy recommends portfolios of **$500+** for their mid-tier plan to justify the $19/month cost.

### How often should crypto portfolios be rebalanced?

**Quarterly rebalancing** provides the optimal balance between tax efficiency and maintaining target allocations for most investors. Monthly rebalancing increases transaction costs by **35-40%** but shows marginal performance improvement of **1-2%** annually. Daily rebalancing is only suitable for portfolios exceeding **$100,000** where tax-loss harvesting benefits outweigh fees. Shrimpy's backtesting shows quarterly outperformed monthly by **0.8%** after accounting for fees in 2023.

### Can rebalancing tools work with hardware wallets?

Yes, but with limitations. **Shrimpy** and **CoinStats** support watch-only wallets for tracking hardware wallet holdings (Ledger, Trezor, Coldcard). However, automated rebalancing requires exchange API connections—hardware wallets must transfer to exchange wallets for execution. **Self-custody rebalancing** is available through **BitHarvest's** DeFi mode for Ethereum and Solana-based assets. For maximum security, use dedicated exchange accounts for automated strategies and cold storage for long-term holdings.

### What fees should I expect when using rebalancing tools?

Total fees include:
- **Platform subscription:** $7.99-$199/month depending on plan
- **Exchange maker/taker fees:** 0.1%-0.5% per trade
- **Network fees:** Variable for on-chain rebalancing ($1-$50 depending on blockchain)
- **Tax implications:** Short-term capital gains (up to 37%) vs. long-term gains (15-20%)

Shrimpy estimates total annual costs of **1.2-2.8%** of portfolio value for monthly rebalancing across two exchanges. CoinStats reports **0.8-1.5%** for quarterly rebalancing on a single exchange.
