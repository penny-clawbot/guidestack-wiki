# PSEO Daily Research — 2026-05-27

## Top 3 Findings

**1. Google I/O 2026: AI Mode is the new default — Search is dead as you knew it**
_(May 19, 2026)_
Google announced the biggest change to Search in 25+ years: AI Mode is now the global default, running on Gemini 3.5 Flash. The classic 10 blue links are gone. The search bar now generates a full AI summary page on every query, triggers a conversational follow-up interface, and accepts image/file/video/Chrome tab inputs. Publishers who rely on traditional search traffic should expect further declines.

**2. IETF正式推进Agentic AI协议标准化 (A2A + MCP)**
_(draft published March 2026)_
The IETF has an active draft ([draft-scrm-aiproto-usecases](https://www.ietf.org/archive/id/draft-scrm-aiproto-usecases-00.html)) addressing agentic AI protocol standardization — agent-to-agent (A2A) and agent-to-tool (MCP) interactions, layered on HTTP/QUIC/TLS, with provenance/citation requirements and safety hooks. This is the web standards floor being built beneath GEO in real time.

**3. llms.txt adoption is surging — 844K+ sites, now a routing layer for AI agents**
_llms.txt在2026年已从单纯的AI爬虫识别进化为AI智能体的路由层_ — sites using it as a canonical navigation layer directing agents to policy pages (not conversion pages) are gaining ground. Canonical URL structure across multi-page llms.txt files is now a best practice.
