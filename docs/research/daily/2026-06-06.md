# GEO/SEO Research Brief - June 6, 2026

## Top 3 Findings

1. **Google AI Mode now serves 1B+ users with Gemini 3.5 Flash**
   Google's AI Mode has surpassed one billion monthly users and is now powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash. The search interface has been completely redesigned with an intelligent search box that supports multimodal inputs and conversational follow-ups directly from AI Overviews.

2. **FAQPage schema officially retired**
   Google officially retired FAQPage structured data on May 7, 2026, making all existing FAQPage markup inert in Google Search results. This ended a nine-year era of Q&A chips in SERPs.

3. **Universal Cart introduces new eligibility model**
   Google's Universal Cart requires a two-part eligibility: native_commerce attribute in Merchant Center feeds AND a UCP profile at /.well-known/ucp. Schema.org Product markup alone is no longer sufficient for Buy button eligibility.
