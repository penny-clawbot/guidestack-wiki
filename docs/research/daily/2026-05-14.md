# GEO/SEO Daily Research — 2026-05-14

## Top Findings

### 1. llms.txt is now critical for AI citation rates
Sites implementing llms.txt see citation attribution jump from 18-32% to 61-74% in AI responses. 600+ sites have adopted it. Vercel reports 10% of signups from ChatGPT. The average LLM search visitor is worth 5.2x more than traditional organic.

**Action:** ✅ Already implemented — llms.txt + llms-full.txt generated at build time.

### 2. GEO is distinct from SEO — content structure matters more than backlinks
AI engines extract structured content first: tables, lists, definitions, figures. A site ranked 15th on Google can be cited in AI Overviews if its content is better structured. Question-style headings ("How to...", "What is...") are strongly preferred.

**Action:** ✅ Build-time validator now checks for data tables, question headings, cited sources, numbered lists, and direct answers in first 150 words.

### 3. FAQPage schema is the highest-impact schema type for AI visibility
FAQ sections with proper FAQPage JSON-LD are directly exploitable by AI engines. Combined with Organization, Article, and BreadcrumbList schemas, they form the foundation of AI discoverability.

**Action:** ✅ FAQPage schema now rendered as separate JSON-LD block on all articles with FAQ sections.
