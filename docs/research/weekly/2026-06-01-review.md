## New Best Practices Discovered

### Content Structure for AI Citation
1. **Direct answers in first 100-150 words** - AI extracts from opening paragraphs
2. **Question-style H2 headings** - "How to...", "What is...", "Why..." for conversational queries
3. **Cited sources with clear attribution** - AI favors content with verifiable data
4. **Conversational formatting** - Content that supports follow-up question paths
5. **Machine-digestible structure** - Content that agents can monitor and cite

### Schema Priorities Shifted
1. **Keep high-impact schemas:** Article, BlogPosting, HowTo, Organization, WebSite, BreadcrumbList
2. **Remove deprecated schemas:** FAQPage (inert), PracticeProblem, Dataset, Course, etc.
3. **Add e-commerce specific:** Product + Offer + AggregateRating stack for Universal Cart eligibility

### AI Crawler Management
1. **Robots.txt compliance:** All major AI bots (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot) respect robots.txt 100%
2. **Traffic patterns:** GPTBot (4,200 hits/day), ClaudeBot (1,800 hits/day), PerplexityBot (980 hits/day)
3. **Crawl budget management:** Rate limiting for AI crawlers now essential
