## Top 4 New Niche Recommendations

### 1. 🏠 Home Insurance Guide
- **Niche:** Home / auto / life insurance education
- **Target domain:** home-insurance-guide.com (or similar)
- **Estimated CPC:** $15-50 (insurance is king of AdSense CPC)
- **Competition:** High CPC but many sites are low-quality affiliate farms — beatable with better content
- **Sample articles:**
  - How to Choose Home Insurance in 2026
  - Auto Insurance Comparison Guide
  - Life Insurance for Beginners: Term vs Whole
  - Best Renters Insurance Plans Compared
  - How to Lower Your Insurance Premiums
- **Why:** Insurance is consistently the #1 highest CPC niche ($15-50/click). Education-focused content with comparison tables is perfect for PSEO. Evergreen demand.
- **Cluster:** Finance (pairs with personal-finance-tips, smart-emergency-fund)

### 2. ⚖️ Legal Self-Help Guide
- **Niche:** Legal topics for individuals and small business
- **Target domain:** legal-self-help-guide.com
- **Estimated CPC:** $20-80 (legal is highest CPC overall)
- **Competition:** Most legal sites are law firm landing pages — low editorial competition
- **Sample articles:**
  - How to File for Bankruptcy: Step by Step
  - Small Business Legal Checklist 2026
  - Understanding Employment Law Basics
  - How to Create a Legal Will Without a Lawyer
  - Trademark vs Copyright: What You Need to Know
- **Why:** Legal has the highest CPC of any niche ($20-80). Informational content ranks well because law firm sites are promotional, not educational. Disclaimer + educational focus = low legal risk.
- **Cluster:** Finance (cross-links with insurance and personal finance)

### 3. 🏥 Health & Wellness Guide
- **Niche:** Health conditions, treatments, nutrition, mental health
- **Target domain:** health-wellness-guide.com
- **Estimated CPC:** $5-15 (health is consistently high)
- **Competition:** Medium — need YMYL-quality content to rank
- **Sample articles:**
  - Understanding Type 2 Diabetes: Complete Guide
  - Best Mental Health Apps 2026
  - Nutrition Basics: Macros, Vitamins, and Minerals
  - How to Improve Sleep Quality Naturally
  - Telehealth vs In-Person: Pros and Cons
- **Why:** Massive search volume, decent CPC, evergreen. Must include medical disclaimers and cite sources (YMYL requirements). Template can handle this with structured data.
- **Cluster:** Lifestyle (pairs with budget-travel-tips)

### 4. 🎓 Online Education Guide
- **Niche:** Online courses, certifications, degree programs, skill development
- **Target domain:** online-education-guide.com
- **Estimated CPC:** $8-25 (education is top-5 CPC niche)
- **Competition:** Low — most education sites are university pages, not comparison content
- **Sample articles:**
  - Best Online MBA Programs 2026
  - Free Online Courses That Actually Matter
  - Google Certifications Worth Getting
  - How to Learn Coding Online for Free
  - Online vs Traditional Degree: ROI Comparison
- **Why:** Education has very high CPC because universities and bootcamps bid aggressively. Perfect for "best of" and comparison articles. Programmatic-friendly with structured course data.
- **Cluster:** Tech (pairs with ai-tools-hub)
